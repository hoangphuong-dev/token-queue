Overview

The Dash Backend Theme is a Modern, Clean, beautiful and Stunning Backend Theme For Your Odoo V17. This Theme Will Give You A New Experience With Odoo and redefined the Odoo UI.

Main Highlight Of The Theme are The New Side Bar with Accordion Sub Menus, Collapsable Side Bar and Card Layout Design. Search Bar placed as a header of View, custom paging component and pre-defined filters shown as Tab Navigation to easily filter your data.

Feel the delightment and the new experience of using Odoo with this clean, stunning, simple and yet familliar and easy to use theme. With this theme you will enjoy staring your screen for hours.

This theme is only works for Odoo 18 Community.
